## How to Play

**1. Fund the Community Fund**
- Send your bet (supported tokens) to the Community Fund wallet (`/list-projects` to get the address).
- Copy your transaction hash.

**2. Create a Challenge**
- Use `/challenge-rps`:
  - `user-tag`: Tag your opponent.
  - `transaction-hash`: Paste your tx hash.
  - (Optional) `memo`: Add a note.
- The bot will announce your challenge and DM your opponent.
- **If your opponent doesnâ€™t join in 30 min, your entry is auto-refunded (with channel + DM notification).**

**3. Join a Challenge**
- Send the same amount to the Community Fund.
- Copy your tx hash.
- Use `/join-rps`:
  - `challenge-id`: Select from active challenges.
  - `transaction-hash`: Paste your tx hash.

**4. Play the Game**
- Once both have joined, use `/play-rps`:
  - `challenge-id`: Select your game.
  - `move`: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
- Both players submit moves each round. Draw? Play again. Winner? Prize is sent!

**5. Winner Gets the Prize!**
- Winner receives the total prize (both bets) to their wallet.
- Winner is announced in the channel and DMed with tx details.
